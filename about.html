About Art Therapy

Strengthening Connections Through Creativity

Music Therapy

Improves mood and reduces agitation, stimulates memory recall through familiar songs, and enhances communication for non-verbal patients.

Visual Art Therapy

Boosts focus, reduces anxiety, enables self-expression when language declines, and strengthens visuospatial and fine motor skills.

Movement Therapy

Improves motor function and mobility, enhances mood through endorphin release, and encourages social engagement while reducing isolation.
